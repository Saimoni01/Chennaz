Tourist Spots

These are the some of the famous spots in Chennai.

  1. Home
  2. Tourist Spots

Marina Beach

Marina Beach Road, Chennai

Marina beach in Chennai along the Bay of Bengal is India's longest and world's second longest beach.

12:00 AM - 11:59 PM; All days

Phoenix Marketcity

Indira Gandhi Nagar, Velachery, Chennai

Watching movies, shopping, food court serving every type of cuisine, fun zone for children, etc everything is available under one roof.

11:00 am to 10:00 pm; Monday to Friday
10:00 am to 10:00 pm; Saturday to Sunday

Vandalur Zoo

Grand Southern Trunk Rd, Vandalur,Chennai.

Arignar Anna Zoological Park, also known as the Vandalur Zoo, is a zoological garden located in Vandalur, is in the southwestern part of Chennai.

9.00 am to 5.00 pm; Monday to Sunday
Tuesday closed

Government Museum

Pantheon Road, Egmore, Chennai

This place is known for preserving some of the best ancient (10-13th century) and modern South Indian bronzes.

9:30 am to 5:30 pm ;All days
closed on Fridays and national holidays

Guindy National Park

Rangeguindy, Chennai.

Home to over 300 kinds of trees and plants, 150 bird species and 15 species of mammals, reptiles and amphibians, Guindy National Park is a forest situated on the grounds that were earlier part of Raj Bhawan and IIT-Madras.

9:00 am to 6:00 pm;All days
closed on Tuesdays

Besant Nagar Beach

Besant Nagar,chennai.

Elliot’s Beach is a famous place to visit with friends or family. This beautiful beach, which was named after Edward Elliot (the Chief Magistrate, Superintendent of Madras during the British Era), is one of the cleanest seafronts in India.

12:00 AM - 11:59 PM; All days
